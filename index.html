The Shiba Inu is a small-to-medium breed of dog originating from Japan. Known for their spirited personality and fox-like appearance, Shibas are one of the oldest and smallest native Japanese dog breeds. They are agile, confident, and possess a strong hunting instinct. Shiba Inus are characterized by their curled tail, erect ears, and a bold demeanor. They are intelligent and independent, making them both loyal companions and a challenge to train. Despite their spirited nature, they are also known for being clean and easily house-trained.

With their charming personality and adorable looks, Shiba Inus have gained popularity worldwide, often referred to as "the doge" due to their viral internet fame.
